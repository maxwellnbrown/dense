331 /*********************************************************************
332  ** mutate **
333  ** ESMutate(population, param) **
334  ** **
335  ** sp_ : copy of sp **
336  ** op_ : copy of op **
337  ** update sp **
338  ** traditional technique using exponential smoothing **
339  ** sp(1->miu-1) : unchanged **
340  ** sp(miu->lambda): sp = sp_*exp(tau_*N(0,1) + tau*Nj(0,1)) **
341  ** Nj : random number generated for each j **
342  ** check sp bound **
343  ** if(sp > bound) then sp = bound **
344  ** differential variation **
345  ** op(1->miu-1) = op_ + gamma*(op_[1] - op_[i+1]) **
346  ** mutation **
347  ** op(miu->lambda): op = op_ +sp * N(0,1) **
348  ** check op bound **
349  ** if(op > ub || op < lb) then try retry times **
350  ** op = op_ + sp * N(0,1) **
351  ** if still not in bound then op = op_ **
352  ** exponential smoothing **
353  ** sp(miu->lambda): sp = sp_ + alpha * (sp - sp_) **
354  ** **
355  ** Master: send op to other processors **
356  ** Slave: re-calculate f/g/phi **
357  *********************************************************************/
358 void ESMutate(ESPopulation *, ESParameter *);
